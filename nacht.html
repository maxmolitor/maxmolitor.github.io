Maximilian Kenzo Molitor
Nachtblumen
Analogue Photography Series on digital Screen; 2023
This photography-series was displayed at the yearly Rundgang-exhibition of the University of the Arts Berlin and dealt with the topic of dissociation between artificial representations and real objects. My analogue photographies captured flowers during night-time with artificial light. Images which are completely separated and dissociated from the actual natural object were produced. They took on an abstract form resembling a painting, animation or a rendering.

The Project was realised in the Photography Course (Summersemester 2023) of Sebastian Herold.
